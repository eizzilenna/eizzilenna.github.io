Drawings

A sample selection of drawings created between 2005 and 2017. Examples from earlier work include large-scale isometric drawings in pencil that map everyday activities (making telephone calls, going shopping) using mathematical algorithms. Later pieces explore different mediums, colours and composition and abandon previous rules-based systems in favour of intuition.
